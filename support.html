❓常见问题

游戏数据丢失了怎么办？

游戏数据保存在您的设备本地。如果您更换了设备或重新安装了应用，本地数据可能无法自动恢复。建议通过 iCloud 备份来保留游戏进度。

如何关闭音效或震动？

进入游戏后，点击设置图标，可以分别开关音效和触感反馈（震动）。

如何使用自定义方块图片？

在方块样式设置中选择"自定义图片"，然后从您的相册中选择一张图片即可。应用仅会使用您选定的图片，不会访问相册中的其他内容。

游戏需要网络连接吗？

不需要。Miko Blocks 完全在本地运行，无需任何网络连接即可正常游戏。

❓Frequently Asked Questions

My game data is gone — what happened?

Game data is saved locally on your device. Switching devices or reinstalling the app may result in data loss. We recommend enabling iCloud backup to preserve your progress.

How do I turn off sound or haptics?

Tap the settings icon in-game to toggle sound effects and haptic feedback on or off independently.

How do I use a custom block image?

In the block style settings, choose "Custom Image" and select a photo from your library. The app only uses the image you select and does not access any other content in your photo library.

Does the game require an internet connection?

No. Miko Blocks runs entirely offline — no internet connection is needed to play.

How can I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Please email us at the address above with a description of the issue or suggestion. We read every message.
